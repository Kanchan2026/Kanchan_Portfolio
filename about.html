About Me

I am a second-year student at JAMK University of Applied Sciences, studying Business Information Technology with a focus on Game Development. I am a motivated UI/UX and Unity UI Designer with a strong interest in designing user interfaces for games and mobile applications.

I enjoy creating clean, intuitive, and visually appealing user interfaces and implementing them directly in Unity. I have hands-on experience with tools such as Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator, and Unity UI systems, where I design and implement menus, HUDs, buttons, and responsive layouts for game projects.

I enjoy collaborating with developers and artists and refining my designs based on feedback. Currently, I am focused on improving my skills in mobile game UI, UI animations, and user-centered design, and I am eager to grow as a UI Artist and UI Designer through real-world projects and practical training opportunities.
